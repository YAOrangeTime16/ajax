Rules of The Games

Point Getter

  1. Draw a pair of cards by clicking the draw-cards button at the buttom of page
  2. You draw cards five times per game. If your cards have a matching number or suit, then you get extra poits.
    1. If both cards have the same number, you get 30 points
    2. If both cards have the same suit, you get 10 points
    3. Otherwise, you get 1 point
  3. Your total points come up after 5 plays (5 clicks)
Play This Game

Twenty One

  1. The aim of this game is to acheive a score 21.
  2. You get a pair of card at the first draw, and then you can add one card or more, as you wish, to get the score close to 21
    1. "Aces" are worth 1 (you cannot choose between 1 and 11 like the blackjack rule)
    2. "Jacks", "Queens", and "Kings" are worth 10
    3. The others count face value
