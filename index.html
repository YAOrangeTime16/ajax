Rules of The Games

Point Getter

  1. Draw a pair of cards by clicking the draw-cards button at the game section
  2. You draw cards five times per game. If your cards have a matching number or suit, then you get extra poits.
    1. If both cards have the same number, you get 30 points
    2. If both cards have the same suit, you get 10 points
    3. Otherwise, you get 1 point
  3. Your total points come up after 5 draws (5 clicks)
Play This Game

Twenty One

  1. The aim of this game is to acheive a score 21.
  2. You get a pair of card with the first draw, and then you can add one card or more, as you wish, to get the score close to 21
    1. "Aces" are worth 11 (you cannot choose between 1 and 11 like the ordinary blackjack rule)
    2. "Jacks", "Queens", and "Kings" are worth 10
    3. The others count face value
  3. When the score hits exact 21, you get Blackjack
  4. If the score excesses over 21, it returns Bust
Play This Game

Five Card Draw

  1. Five Card Draw is a variant of poker games
  2. You get five cards in your hand with a click of "Start"
  3. Choose cards which you wish to replace with new cards, then click "Draw"
  4. "Result" shows the result of your hand
    1. Royal flush (less than 0.001%)
    2. Straight flush (less than 0.002%)
    3. Four of a kind (0.02%)
    4. Full house (0.14%)
    5. Flush (0.20%)
    6. Straight (0.39%)
    7. Three of a kind (2.11%)
    8. Two pair (4.75%)
    9. Pair (42.30%)
    10. High card (50.10%)

(%) stands for frequency of 5-card poker hands (source from wikipedia)
